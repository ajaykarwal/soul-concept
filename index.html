About Soul Concept

I've been DJing since around 1996 and have played all sorts of styles over the years.. Hiphop, UK Garage, Bhangra, Pop, House and of course Drum and Bass...

These days I produce and play mainly liquid / soulful / atmospheric Drum and Bass. My sound is relatively upbeat and melodic, with warm basslines and crisp breaks.

I'm heavily influenced by the mid-2000's liquid drum and bass scene and fell in love with the genre due to artist such as Calibre, Makoto, High Contrast, Zero T, Lenzman, LTJ Bukem, Redeyes, Alix Perez, Krust, DJ Marky and Commix to name a few.

I also occasionally incorporate my Indian heritage into my music, fusing it with drum and bass to create music that is inspired by the likes of Osmani Soundz, Nitin Sawhney, Karsh Kale, Talvin Singh, Shiva Soundsystem and The Nasha Experience.
